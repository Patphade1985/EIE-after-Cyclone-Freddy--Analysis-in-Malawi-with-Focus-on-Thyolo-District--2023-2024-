
EDUCATION CANNOT WAIT - CYCLONE FREDDY RESPONSE
================================================
THYOLO DISTRICT EDUCATION IMPACT ANALYSIS
Analysis Date: 2025-12-13

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
Thyolo District represents a moderate-impact area requiring targeted Education in 
Emergencies (EiE) intervention. With 50 affected schools (7.9% of national total) 
and US$1.78 million recovery needs (4.2% of national), Thyolo warrants inclusion 
in ECW's first response phase.

QUANTITATIVE FINDINGS
---------------------
• Schools affected: 50 (43 primary, 7 secondary)
• Education recovery needs: US$1.78 million
• Total disaster effects: US$35.4 million
• Displaced persons: 3,759 in 36 IDP camps
• Fatalities: 13, Injuries: 65

COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS
--------------------
• Thyolo proportion: 7.9% of schools, 4.2% of funding needs
• Cost efficiency: US$35,600 per school (vs national US$66,500)
• Gender balance: 51% girls among affected learners

ECW PROGRAMMATIC IMPLICATIONS
-----------------------------
1. Response Level: Moderate priority
2. Target beneficiaries: 57,300+ learners
3. Key interventions: Temporary learning spaces, WASH rehabilitation
4. Budget requirement: US$1.78 million
5. Timeframe: Immediate (2024 academic year)
